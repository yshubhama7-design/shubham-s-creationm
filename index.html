DU SOL – BA CA (Major CS, Minor Mathematics) Syllabus

Unit 1 — Limits, Continuity & Differentiability

  • Definition of limit, algebra of limits
  • Existence of limit (left/right limits)
  • Definition of continuity, kinds of discontinuity
  • Definition of derivability, continuity vs differentiability

Unit 2 — Successive Differentiation

  • nth derivative rules
  • Standard nth derivative results
  • nth derivative using partial fractions
  • Leibniz's theorem

Unit 3 — Taylor & Maclaurin Expansions

  • Taylor’s theorem with remainder
  • Maclaurin’s theorem
  • Series expansion of standard functions
  • Taylor series in powers of (x − a)
  • Using differentiation & integration for expansions

Unit 4 — Asymptotes of Curves

  • Asymptotes parallel to axes
  • Oblique asymptotes
  • Asymptotes of algebraic curves
  • Intersection of curve & its asymptotes
  • Asymptotes of polar curves

Unit 5 — Curvature & Curve Geometry

  • Definition & basic concepts of curvature
  • Radius of curvature (general & Newton’s method)
  • Centre & circle of curvature
  • Transforming polar equation to pedal equation
  • Chord of curve parallel to axes
  • Symmetry & nature of origin

Unit 6 — Curve Tracing

  • Curve tracing in Cartesian form
  • Parametric curve tracing
  • Identifying behaviour, tangents, symmetry

Unit 7 — Integral Calculus & Reduction Formulae

  • Reduction formulas for integrals
  • Standard reduction patterns

Unit 8 — Rectification (Arc Length)

  • Arc length in Cartesian form
  • Arc length in parametric form
  • Length of cycloid
  • Arc length in polar form
  • Arc length in pedal form
  • Intrinsic equations

Unit 9 — Quadrature (Area)

  • Area between curves
  • Area between curve & its asymptotes
  • Area of parametric curves
  • Area of polar curves
  • Area between two polar curves
