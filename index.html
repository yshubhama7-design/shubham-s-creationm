DU SOL – BA CA (Major CS, Minor Mathematics) Syllabus

Lesson 1 — Limits

  • Formal definition of limit
  • Left-hand & right-hand limits
  • Limit laws and theorems
  • Standard limits (trigonometric, exponential, logarithmic)

Lesson 2 — Continuity & Differentiability

  • Definition of continuity
  • Types of discontinuity
  • Differentiability & its relation to continuity
  • Algebra of continuous and differentiable functions

Lesson 3 — Successive Differentiation

  • nth derivative rules
  • Standard nth derivative results
  • Partial fraction method for nth derivative
  • Leibniz’s theorem for nth derivative

Lesson 4 — Partial Differentiation

  • Partial derivatives (first and higher order)
  • Total derivative
  • Homogeneous functions & Euler’s theorem
  • Chain rule for multivariable functions

Lesson 5 — Mean Value Theorem

  • Rolle’s theorem
  • Lagrange’s Mean Value Theorem (LMVT)
  • Cauchy’s Mean Value Theorem (CMVT)
  • Geometric interpretation

Lesson 6 — Extremum & Convergence of Series

  • Maxima and minima of functions
  • Second derivative test
  • Series and sequences
  • Tests for convergence

Lesson 7 — Indeterminate Forms

  • 0/0, ∞/∞ forms
  • 0 × ∞, ∞ − ∞ forms
  • 0⁰, 1⁰, ∞⁰ forms
  • L'Hôpital’s rule

Lesson 8 — Taylor’s & Maclaurin’s Series

  • Taylor’s theorem with remainder
  • Maclaurin expansions
  • Series of standard functions
  • Using derivatives for generating series

Lesson 9 — Reduction Formulae for Integrals

  • Reduction formula for ∫sinⁿx dx, ∫cosⁿx dx
  • Reduction for ∫tanⁿx dx
  • Reduction for ∫xⁿeˣ dx, ∫xⁿsinx dx
  • Application to definite integrals

Lesson 10 — Asymptotes, Concavity & Point of Inflection

  • Asymptotes parallel to axes
  • Oblique asymptotes
  • Concavity & convexity
  • Inflection points
  • Monotonicity & curvature intro

Lesson 11 — Tracing of Curves

  • Curve tracing in Cartesian form
  • Symmetry, intercepts, tangents
  • Behaviour at infinity
  • Parametric curve tracing
  • Polar curve tracing
